My Vacation Spot: Japan

Why Japan?

I would love to visit Japan because it combines modern innovation with deep cultural history. As someone interested in technology and design, I admire Japan's leadership in robotics, transportation, and architecture. At the same time, I'm fascinated by its temples, traditions, and landscapes.

Places I Want to Visit

  • Tokyo – Explore Akihabara's tech scene, visit robotics exhibits, and ride the bullet train.
  • Kyoto – Visit traditional temples, gardens, and historical districts.
  • Osaka – Enjoy street food and explore the Dotonbori nightlife.
  • Mount Fuji – See Japan’s most famous mountain and possibly hike it.
  • Hiroshima – Learn about its history and visit the Peace Memorial Park.

Why It Connects to My Interests

As a future mechanical engineer, I’m inspired by Japan’s work in robotics and precision engineering. I would love to see how companies like Toyota, Honda, and Sony innovate with mechanical systems. Visiting Japan would let me experience these technologies up close while also learning from a culture that values discipline, creativity, and attention to detail.
